Props Assistant / Driver

Da Vinci's Demons Season Two - Starz / BBC Worldwide 2013 - 14

This role involved working to support the art department of this major television series. Handling mainly "action" props such as swords, shields, and various other weaponry, I was responsible for the safe transport of these often very valuable items to and from sets, location shoots, and prop hire companies.

It was also part of my job to read the script and produce a breakdown of potential prop requirements cross referenced with the shooting schedule to determine when each prop was required. I would then compare notes with the storeman, buyers, food hygienist, and props master to plan what was needed when and if anything needed fabricating or hiring, and make plans to ensure everything arrived on time.

In television production it is imperative each department plans its activities very carefully to ensure all requirements of the shoot are fully met as even a few hours delay to a shoot can cost tens of thousands of pounds. Working in the action props team we took great pride in ensuring all our props arrived on set on time looking perfect with no mistakes.

Other Interests

Surfing
